About Me

Hello, I hope you are doing well.

I graduated from Brown University with a Master of Science in Computer Science in May 2024 and a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics-Computer Science in May 2023.

My primary professional expertise lies in backend software engineering, particularly in databases, networking, and low-latency systems.

I am most passionate about cosmology. Studying the history, structure, and future of our universe is something I never tire of, and it continually deepens my appreciation for the natural world around us.

I enjoy hiking, camping, chess, and visiting museums. I am an avid reader, particularly of history, science fiction, and current events. I love watching the Champions and Premier League, as well as playing soccer.
